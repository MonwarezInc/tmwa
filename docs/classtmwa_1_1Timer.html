tmwa::Timer::Timer ( tick_t  tick,
timer_func  func,
interval_t  interval = interval_t::zero() 
)

Schedule a timer for the given tick. If you do not wish to keep track of it, call disconnect(). Otherwise, you may cancel() or replace (operator =) it later.

If the interval argument is given, the timer will reschedule itself again forever. Otherwise, it will disconnect() itself just BEFORE it is called.

Cancel the delivery of this timer's function, and make it falsy. Implementation note: this doesn't actually remove it, just sets the functor to do_nothing, and waits for the tick before removing.
